## Content Strategy for LinkedIn

### Topics That Attract Target Recruiters

1. **Data Engineering technical content** (dbt, Spark, Airflow, Snowflake)
2. **Remote work culture posts** — your experience as a remote data engineer
3. **LATAM tech ecosystem posts** — show you understand the market
4. **Data architecture diagrams** — demonstrate expertise visually
5. **Spain/Portugal/Europe tech posts** — show interest in the EU market

### Posting Frequency
- Minimum: 2x per week
- Optimal: 4x per week (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri)
- Never post promotional "hire me" content — post expertise instead

## Red Flags to Avoid

- Sending generic connection requests without a note
- Connecting with HR professionals outside your target markets
- Accepting every inbound connection (dilutes algorithm relevance)
- Being passive — posting content drives 10x more inbound recruiter contact
- Ignoring your existing high-priority contacts (206 contacts with score >=70)
