Data Quality & Limitations

Understanding what LinkedIn exports — and what it doesn't.

⚠️ UNKNOWN does NOT mean irrelevant. LinkedIn's bulk export only includes: Name, URL, Company, Position, Connected On. Location is never exported. UNKNOWN = no geographic keyword found — not worthless.

Why is UNKNOWN so high?

A company named "Digital Ventures Solutions" gives zero geographic signal even if it operates in Austin, TX. Only if the company name or title contains a recognizable geographic keyword can the inference engine assign a market.
